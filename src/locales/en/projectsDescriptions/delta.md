**Delta** is a comprehensive corporate web platform built for a major commercial leasing company. The platform is designed to represent the business across 9 key industrial sectors while driving high-converting B2B lead generation through specialized catalogs, real-time calculation modules, and regional branching infrastructure.

### My Role & Key Contributions (Frontend)

As a Frontend Developer, I engineered the interactive user interfaces, implemented dynamic layout switches, and constructed the client-side financial estimation engines.

#### Key Implemented Modules & Features:

- **Interactive Leasing Calculator:**
  - Developed a real-time calculation widget utilizing custom slider inputs for property value, advance payment percentage, and contract duration.
  - Triggered instant client-side updates for monthly payments, VAT returns, and yearly depreciation metrics.

- **Automated Geolocation Tracking:**
  - Programmed client-side scripts to capture user location data via geolocation services.
  - Dynamically updated corresponding regional contact numbers, addresses, and branch details across global headers and footers.

- **Dynamic Financial Report Grids:**
  - Created responsive data tables with tabbed navigation, allowing users to toggle smoothly between annual fiscal KPI metrics and archived balance documents for previous periods.

- **Multi-Tiered Industry & Partner Directories:**
  - Built catalog pages grouping 9 industries and corporate partners, featuring popover asset descriptions via logo clicks.
  - Developed case study news feeds with custom image sliders and pre-owned machinery lists.

- **Validated Lead Capture Forms:**
  - Engineered complex conversion forms for structural leasing inquiries, callback requests, and HR resume drops.
  - Equipped forms with conditional client-side field validation rules and integrated visual CAPTCHA verification blocks.

- **Map Interactivity & Mobile Refinement:**
  - Integrated Yandex.Maps API for pinpointing national branch networks in various Russian cities.
  - Performed strict responsive cross-browser optimizations to maintain smooth numeric form entry across desktop and smartphone viewports (Pixel Perfect alignment).
